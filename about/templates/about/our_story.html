Our Commitment to Eco-Friendly & Organic Principles

At the heart of KK39 Candles lies a deep respect for both our planet and your well-being. We believe in the power of natural candles, which is why we exclusively use sustainable, natural waxes like soy and, where possible, organic ingredients. Our dedication means you can enjoy a clean burning candle experience, free from the harmful toxins often found in conventional options. We are proud to offer non-toxic candles that are kinder to you and the environment.

Pet-Friendly Considerations: A Gentle Glow for All

We love our furry family members too! We understand that pets can have sensitivities, especially to strong fragrances. That's why we proudly offer an option to make any candle you like scent-free. These pet-friendly candles provide the same warm glow and beautiful aesthetic without any added essential oils or fragrance oils, making them a wonderful choice for homes with sensitive companions. You can create a calming atmosphere with our natural unscented candles, knowing they are a gentler option for your pets.

* While many of our carefully curated scented eco candles use pure, high-quality essential oils, every pet is different. We always recommend observing your pets when introducing any new scent into their environment and ensuring good ventilation.
