Our Commitment to Quality Ingredients

At KK39 Candles, we believe that true luxury lies in purity and intention. The beautiful glow and captivating aromas of our handmade candles, room sprays, and diffusers begin with an unwavering commitment to high-quality, natural ingredients. We transparently share what goes into our creations, so you can feel confident and serene when bringing our products into your home.

The Foundation: Premium, Eco-Friendly Waxes

High-Quality Soy Wax: For our container candles, we choose premium soy wax, a natural and renewable resource Candle Shack. This eco-friendly wax is renowned for its clean burn, producing significantly less soot than traditional paraffin waxes. It also offers an excellent scent throw, beautifully carrying the natural oils we infuse.

Specialized Soy Wax Blend for Pillar Candles: Crafting the perfect pillar candle requires a wax that offers both structural integrity and a beautiful burn. Our high-quality soy wax blend is the quality of your products. This carefully selected blend maintains the eco-friendly benefits of soy while providing the necessary structure and stability for a longer, more consistent burn in standalone candles. It allows our handmade pillar candles to be both beautiful and functional.

The Perfect Flame: Organic Hemp Wicks

A critical component for a clean-burning candle is its wick. We are proud to use organic hemp wicks in all our candles. Hemp is a wonderfully sustainable crop, and our organic wicks are designed to provide a steady, consistent flame with minimal smoke or soot. They are free from lead and other harmful metals, contributing to a healthier ambiance in your home.

Captivating Aromas: Pure & Natural Oils

The delightful scents that fill your space from our scented candles, room sprays, and diffusers are derived from high-quality, natural oils. We steer clear of harsh synthetic fragrances, opting instead for a blend of essential oils and naturally-derived fragrance oils. This commitment ensures our aromatherapy home products and scented goods deliver authentic, uplifting, and non-toxic scents that enhance your environment naturally.

*All our oils and fragrances are phthalate-free, paraben-free, cruelty-free.

Artisan Vessels: Handmade Eco Jesmonite & Quality Glass

We believe your candle should be a piece of home decor even before it's lit. That's why, apart from classic, recyclable glass jars, many of our unique candle containers are handmade by us using eco-friendly Jesmonite. Jesmonite is a versatile, water-based composite material that is durable, VOC-free, and allows us to create stunning, tactile vessels with a contemporary matte finish. Each handmade Jesmonite jar is crafted with love and extra care, making it a distinctive and sustainable container you can cherish or repurpose.

Our Promise

From our natural soy waxes and organic hemp wicks to our natural-essential oils and handmade Jesmonite vessels, every element is chosen with purpose. We are dedicated to offering you high-quality eco candles and home fragrance products that are as kind to the earth and your home (including being mindful of pet-friendly unscented options) as they are beautiful to behold.
